Making the Decision To Switch

You should only move forward with Canvas if and when you have determined that we far outperform other vendors and offer an unmatched return on investment. Our goal is to give you as much information as possible to make this determination one way or the other. We will provide access to a sandvox of the Canvas application for you to get a feel for the platform as well as references to current customers to talk about their experience.

2.

Interfaces and Community Intergration

Canvas is meant to integrate with you exisiting interfaces and community resources. At a minimum, this means porting over you fax number, enrolling you payers with our clearinghouse, and getting bidirectional lab feed running. We'll also load your preferred specialists and imaging centers into Canvas so that sending referrals and closing loops is seamless. Finally, if interfaces with health information exchanges or other information technology vendors are important, we will work with you to determine what we can deliver and when.

3.

Data Migration

The first thing we will do is gain access to a full backup of your current data, which we have executed successfully with both remotely hosted and on-premise EMR databases. Then our work begins to import into Canvas. Patient demographics and information like medications, problems, and allergies is relatively straightforward. While importing clinical notes and documents is more challenging, out team has been able to do this at a high level of fidelity with a wide variety or EMRs.
